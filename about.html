Kiraku...?

Kiraku (気楽) is a Japanese word that means "at ease", "relaxed" or "unconstrained". It is composed of the kanjis 気 (ki), which means "spirit" or "energy", and 楽 (raku), which means "pleasure" or "comfort". The word can be used to describe a feeling of tranquility or relaxation in relation to a situation or task.
